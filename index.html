Customs

Best Route: Start at the western spawn point near Big Red, move through Dorms for high-value loot, and exit through the ZB-1011 extraction point.

Interchange

Best Route: Enter through the front entrance, loot Techlight and Mantis, and exit through Emercom checkpoint.

Shoreline

Best Route: Move from the southern spawn, loot the Resort, and extract at Tunnel or Road to Customs.

Reserve

Best Route: Start at the underground bunker, move to Black Bishop, and extract at the D-2 Bunker or Cliff Descent.

How to Deploy the Site on GitHub Pages

  1. Create a GitHub repository and name it something like 'tarkov-map-routes'.
  2. Inside the repository, upload the HTML file, along with 'styles.css' and an 'images' folder containing the map images.
  3. In GitHub, go to 'Settings' of your repository.
  4. Scroll down to 'Pages' in the left-hand sidebar.
  5. Under 'Branch', select 'main' or 'master' and then click on '/' (root) for the directory.
  6. Click 'Save'. GitHub will provide a link where your site will be published (usually something like 'https://yourusername.github.io/tarkov-map-routes').
  7. Refresh after a few minutes to see your deployed website live!
